## App Store Description

**LanNote Sync — Private Notes, Local Sharing**

*Zero cloud. Zero accounts. Zero tracking. Infinite privacy.*

Share notes with your team at the speed of light — no internet, no servers, no subscriptions. LanNote Sync works completely offline using your local WiFi network.

**🔒 PRIVACY FIRST**
Your notes never leave your device unencrypted. End-to-end encrypted with AES-256-GCM and RSA-2048 key exchange. Unlike Evernote, Notion, or Apple Notes — we NEVER have access to your notes.

**📴 AIRPLANE MODE COLLABORATION**
Working in a basement? Dead WiFi zone? Remote location? LanNote Sync uses mDNS (Bonjour) to discover teammates on the same network — no internet required.

**👋 SHAKE TO DISCOVER**
Just shake your phone to find teammates nearby. Haptic feedback confirms discovery. Notes fly between devices in seconds.

**🏢 AUTO-JOIN MEETING ROOMS**
Connect to "Meeting Room Alpha" WiFi → automatically join the collaboration room. See 8 teammates. 47 shared notes. All synced, all live.

**🎙️ LOCAL VOICE-TO-TEXT**
Record voice notes transcribed 100% on-device. No Siri. No Google. No internet. 95%+ accuracy using on-device ML.

**🔮 AR WHITEBOARD CAPTURE**
Point camera at whiteboards, sticky notes, or handwriting → AI converts to editable markdown notes instantly.

**📲 NFC TAP-TO-SHARE**
Tap phones together for instant key exchange and note bundle sharing. One tap = secure connection forever.

**⚡ BLAZING FAST**
Notes synced in under 2 seconds. Watch the speed counter: "Shared 12 notes in 1.3s 👀"
